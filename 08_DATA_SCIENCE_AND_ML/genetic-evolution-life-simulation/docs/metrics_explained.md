# Metrics explained

Population is the number of living agents.

Food is the number of food items currently available.

Average energy shows the mean internal energy of living agents.

Average speed is the mean inherited movement speed.

Average perception is the mean inherited sensing radius.

Average metabolism is the mean baseline energy cost.

Average mutation is the mean mutation intensity trait.

Average generation shows how many reproduction steps separate current agents from initial agents.

Oldest age shows the oldest living agent.

Best fitness proxy is not a real external fitness score. It combines energy, eaten food, children and age as a rough diagnostic.
