# Controls and usage

Run with:

python3 main.py

Controls:

Space pauses or resumes the simulation.

N advances one simulation step when paused.

R resets the world.

F adds a burst of food.

A adds a burst of agents.

1 selects balanced ecosystem.

2 selects scarce food ecosystem.

3 selects abundant food ecosystem.

4 selects high mutation ecosystem.

5 selects drift ecosystem.

T toggles trails.

D toggles debug overlay.

S saves a screenshot.

Q or Escape exits.
