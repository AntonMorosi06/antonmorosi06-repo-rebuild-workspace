# Issue 003 - MicroBot swarm mapping

## Goal

Map the artificial-life simulation to future MicroBot swarm experiments.

## Future work

Map agents to simulated MicroBots.

Map food to energy/charging points.

Map perception to sensor radius.

Map genome to controller parameters.

Map reproduction to strategy replication, not physical reproduction.

Map death to deactivation or failure state.

## Acceptance criteria

The mapping should remain clearly conceptual unless validated with real hardware.
