# Style presets

## cinematic

Minor/cinematic scale, moderate tempo, layered chords, bass, pad and simple dramatic drums.

## ambient

Slower tempo, softer velocities, longer note durations and gentler harmonic background.

## arcade

Faster tempo, shorter rhythms, brighter velocities and more active drum pattern.

## dark

Phrygian color, slower tempo, lower movement and darker percussion feel.
