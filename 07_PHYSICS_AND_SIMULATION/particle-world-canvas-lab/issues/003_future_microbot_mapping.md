# Issue 003 - Future MicroBot mapping

## Goal

Map the particle simulation to MicroBot swarm visualization.

## Future work

Treat particles as agents.

Treat attractors as target points.

Treat repulsors as obstacles.

Treat color as energy, role or state.

Treat trails as telemetry history.

Add communication radius overlays.

## Acceptance criteria

The future MicroBot mapping should distinguish clearly between abstract visualization and hardware-validated robot behavior.
