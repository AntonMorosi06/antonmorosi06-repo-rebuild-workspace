# Controls and usage

Run locally with:

python3 -m http.server 8080

Open:

http://127.0.0.1:8080

Controls:

Space pauses or resumes.

R resets.

B creates a burst.

T toggles trails.

D toggles debug overlay.

1 selects calm orbit.

2 selects vortex.

3 selects gravity well.

4 selects repulsion field.

5 selects swarm drift.

6 selects chaos mode.

Move the mouse over the canvas to attract or repel particles. Click the canvas to create a burst.
