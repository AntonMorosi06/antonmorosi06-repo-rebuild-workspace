# Visual model

The renderer uses several visual layers.

The background contains a generated starfield.

The accretion region is shown with circular glow rings.

Particles become hotter in color as their energy increases near the black hole.

Trails show recent particle paths.

Shockwaves appear when particles are absorbed.

Polar jets are drawn as translucent beams above and below the central object.

The event horizon is represented as a dark circular region with a bright rim.

All of these effects are visual design devices. They are intended to communicate the idea of attraction, orbit, absorption and emission.
